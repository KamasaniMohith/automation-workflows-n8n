# 📊 SmartReport — Automated Weekly Report

> Runs every Monday at 8 AM sharp and delivers a full business report — automatically!

---

## ✨ What It Does

Every Monday morning at 8 AM, this workflow:
- Triggers on a precise schedule (no manual effort)
- Pulls data from your connected sources
- Compiles and formats a clean weekly report
- Sends the report via email to the right people

Your team wakes up to a fresh report in their inbox — every single week.

---

## 🛠️ Tools & Tech

| Tool | Purpose |
|------|---------|
| n8n | Workflow automation |
| Schedule Trigger | Monday 8 AM trigger |
| Google Sheets | Data source |
| Email / Gmail | Report delivery |


## 🚀 How to Use

1. Import `workflow.json` into your n8n instance
2. Connect your Google Sheets and Gmail credentials
3. Update the Sheet ID and email recipients in the nodes
4. Activate — your Monday reports are now on autopilot!
